Road to the final

potential futures — who Austria could meet each round

Austria scraped through Group J in the most nerve-shredding fashion imaginable — Kalajdzic's equaliser deep in stoppage time salvaging a 3-3 draw with Algeria after Mahrez had twice turned the game on its head, the second time with three minutes left on the clock. They advance as group runners-up, but the performance offered little comfort ahead of what comes next. Spain await in the Round of 32 on Thu Jul 2 12:00 PT, a formidable proposition for any side and a near-mountain for an Austria team that has looked shakily porous at the back; the Spaniards arrive as heavy favourites and will expect to control the tie from first whistle. Survive that, and Croatia or Portugal lurk in the Round of 16 — either one a further stern examination of whether this squad has genuine knockout-stage substance.
