Cape Verde took Argentina to extra time in the Round of 32, twice drawing level before Borges's 111th-minute strike ended their resistance — a magnificent effort from a side that had ground out three draws to qualify from Group H. Duarte's equaliser just after the hour and Cabral's reply to Martínez's 92nd-minute sucker punch showed real character, but the quality gap eventually told. They depart the tournament with their reputation considerably enhanced, having pushed the world champions closer than almost anyone expected.
